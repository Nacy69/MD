## Business Segments

NovaTech operates across three reportable segments:

### 1. Cloud Infrastructure Services

**Revenue: $1.89B (+18% YoY)**

Our flagship cloud platform, **NovaCloud**, serves over 4,800 enterprise clients across hybrid and multi-cloud environments. Key achievements in 2025:

- Launched **NovaCloud Edge** — distributed computing nodes in 14 new metro regions
- Achieved **ISO 27001**, **SOC 2 Type II**, and **FedRAMP High** certifications
- Renewed multi-year agreements with three of the top five U.S. banks

### 2. AI & Data Platform

**Revenue: $1.64B (+34% YoY)**

The fastest-growing segment. The **NovaMind AI Suite** provides enterprise-grade machine learning operations, generative AI tooling, and real-time analytics.

- **NovaMind** surpassed 2,100 active enterprise deployments as of Q4 2025
- Introduced **NovaMind Agents** — autonomous workflow automation for finance, HR, and legal verticals
- Strategic partnership with three leading hyperscale cloud providers for native integrations

### 3. Cybersecurity Solutions

**Revenue: $1.18B (+14% YoY)**

**NovaShield** delivers zero-trust network architecture, endpoint detection & response (EDR), and AI-driven threat intelligence.

- Blocked over **14.2 billion** threat events across the customer network in 2025
- Expanded managed security services (MSSS) to 22 new countries
- Acquired **Cipherion Inc.** for $310M to bolster identity and access management capabilities

## Risk Factors

Management has identified the following principal risks to the business:

**Macroeconomic Conditions**
Slower enterprise IT spending in a rising-rate environment could impact deal velocity and average contract values.

**Cybersecurity & Data Privacy**
As a security vendor, NovaTech is itself a high-value target. Any significant breach could damage reputation and result in regulatory penalties.

**Competition**
The AI and cloud markets are intensely competitive, with well-capitalized hyperscalers and nimble startups competing across all three segments.

**Talent Acquisition**
Demand for AI/ML engineers and cloud architects significantly outpaces supply. Failure to attract and retain talent could impair product roadmap execution.

**Regulatory Environment**
Increasing AI regulation across the EU, U.S., and Asia Pacific creates compliance complexity and potential product modification requirements.

**Geopolitical Risk**
Operations in 38 countries expose NovaTech to export controls, data sovereignty laws, and regional instability.

### Strategic Priorities

**1. AI Platform Leadership**
Double down on NovaMind as the enterprise AI platform of choice. Expand vertical-specific AI agents across financial services, healthcare, and public sector.

**2. Global Expansion**
Target $1B+ revenue from Asia Pacific by 2027. Open new offices in Tokyo, Singapore, and Sydney; establish a regional data center in Mumbai.

**3. Platform Consolidation**
Accelerate cross-segment integration of NovaCloud, NovaMind, and NovaShield into a unified **NovaPlatform** experience — reducing friction and increasing customer lifetime value.

**4. M&A Acceleration**
With $2.1B in cash and equivalents, NovaTech will pursue strategic acquisitions in AI infrastructure, vertical SaaS, and identity security.

**5. Sustainability**
Commit to **carbon neutrality by 2030** across all owned operations. Transition 100% of data center power to renewable sources by 2027.
